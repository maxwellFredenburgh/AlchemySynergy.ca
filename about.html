About Us

Our formulary technology scope includes working with intermediate chemicals, beneficial microbes, enzymes, polymeric antimicrobials, disinfectants, plastics, coatings, adhesives, polymers, polarized polymers, foams, films, fibers and fabrics... to name a few

Our process application experience is almost as broad as our formulary experience.

We draw upon, use and create from all raw material resources including polyurethane, chloroprene, epoxy, EPS, silanes, rubbers (synthetic and natural), acrylics, nylons, polyester and polypropylene, PLA and fluorochemicals... again, to name a few.

Our formulations and proprietary synthesized polymers have commercial uses in specialty print technologies, UV curable systems, foam manufacturing, nonwovens manufacturing, packaging, electronics, farming processes, iodour control and coated thin film products... to name a few!

And we occassional work on a few top secret projects that we aren’t at liberty to disclose…

We provide a unique development philosophy that allows us to not just “think outside the box” but to “be outside of the box”.

Often creating and implementing solutions for our clients that come from other industries…

In our experience Innovative Technology is the key to longevity and sustainable profitability... and in tough markets Innovative Technology is essential element for survival.

So what can we do for you? Drop us an email on the contact link above if you want to connect.
